Game Developer

Information

A Game Developer is a software professional who designs, builds, and maintains video games for various platforms such as PC, consoles (PlayStation, Xbox, Nintendo), and mobile (Android, iOS). They write the underlying code, work with game engines like Unity or Unreal Engine, and collaborate with artists, designers, and sound engineers to bring interactive experiences to life.
