Embedded Systems

Information

Embedded systems are specialized computing systems that are integrated into hardware to perform dedicated tasks. Unlike general-purpose computers, embedded systems are optimized for performance, reliability, and efficiency within tight hardware constraints.


They power everything from smartwatches and microwaves to cars, robots, and satellites.
